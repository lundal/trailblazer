Attention!

Trailblazer requires a new layout feature called css3-grid-layout which is experimental and disabled by default in most browsers.

If you see a rainbow below, all is well!

If you don't see a rainbow, follow these steps:

NB! The links must be copied or typed into your address bar!

  1. Enter chrome://flags into your address bar
  2. Enable Experimental Web Platform features
  3. Restart your browser
  1. Enter about:config into your address bar
  2. Toggle layout.css.grid.enabled
  3. Reload this page
